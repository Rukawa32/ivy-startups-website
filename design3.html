Why IvyStartups?

IvyStartups Taiwan began as a handful of alumni and friends meeting in coffee shops around Taipei, trading notes on fundraising, hiring, and cross-border moves. We are designing the space we wish we had when we first landed in Taiwan.

  • Warm intros to relevant investors
